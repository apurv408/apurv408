Mahindra Cars


Mahindra car price starts at Rs 6.13 Lakh for the cheapest model which is KUV100 NXT and the price of most expensive model, which is Alturas G4 starts at Rs 28.77 Lakh. Mahindra offers 9 car models in India, including 4 cars in SUV category, 1 car in MUV category, 4 cars in Compact SUV category. Mahindra has 5 upcoming cars in India, e20 NXT, S204, eXUV300, New Scorpio and eKUV100.

Mahindra and Mahindra, which was formerly known as Muhammad and Mahindra, was established in 1945. The company, which has its headquarters in Mumbai, is a part of the Mahindra Group, an Indian conglomerate.

The company has factories located in Nashik, Mumbai, and Pune, all in the state of Maharashtra. The carmaker currently sells models such as the KUV100 NXT, Bolero, Bolero Neo, Marazzo, Scorpio, Alturas G4, XUV300, Thar, and the latest entrant, the XUV700.

Also in the works from the brand are a few other models such as the new-gen Scorpio, five-door Thar, all-new Bolero, all-new XUV300, as well as hybrid and fully electric models in the next five years.
